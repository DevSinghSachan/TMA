The original paper on the Dirichlet compound multinomial (DCM) and burstiness is
R. Madsen, D. Kauchak, and C. Elkan. Modeling Word Burstiness Using the Dirichlet Distribution . In Proceedings of the Twenty-Second International Conference on Machine Learning (ICML'05).
http://cseweb.ucsd.edu/~elkan/icml2005.pdf
There is a detailed explanation of the intuitions connecting the DCM and burstiness in this paper. If anyone has further questions or comments,
they are very welcome.
Charles Elkan
