In my Gibbs sampler code, I did the following:
Given corpus partitioned into two groups of documents,
I. Train model on group 1.
II. Sample group 2 while leaving group 1 fixed.
IIa. For just one iteration.
IIb. For several iterations.
III. Sample both groups together on each iteration.
