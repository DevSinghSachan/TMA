Gibbs/MCMC performance measures are called
"diagnostics" in the statistical literature.
Its an art, not a science.  Probably good enough
to look at the training set log likelihood,
the standard value that includes all the latent
topic assignments.
Measuring (provably) unbiased likelihood that
marginalises out the latent topic assignments,
is described in my Asian Machine Learning Conf.
paper in 2009,
based on some breakthrough techniques from Hanna Wallach's PhD.
